About Us

The main purpose of the group is to help the needy, to provide support to people who don't earn a lot. There are 3 things attached to these group:
*Hospital- there is a building for hospital, where patients could be treated and can come to make complaint about how they are feeling. The hospital is fully equiped with all instrument needed for performing surgeries, the hospital has well trained Doctors and Nurses.
*Home for the homeless- There are a lot of people who have nowhere to live, Good Deeds has built differnt homes all around the world for people who need where to live without charging them at all.
*school-Children need to go to school right?,this group has made it possible for children from kindergaten to High School to school at a very low cost. The school has different activities for the students, it has lot of things that will get the attention of the students and make them feel happy.
