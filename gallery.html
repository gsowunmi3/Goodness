Online Classes

About

We offer online classes for students within the age range of 6 and 16. We would have classes thrice a week. The student will have to choose 6 subjects. Each day, the student will take two subjects. Every two weekend, there will be an assessment test to see how well the student understands what is taught.
Our classes are taught by experienced and qualified teachers. The teachers have up to five years of experience in teaching.
